Home Photo Bank

  • Home Photo Bank – a safe place to store photos on your home network
  • Photos are always JPEGs – if not, convert them first using your photo software
  • Photos are organised by creation date (from EXIF data)
  • Named photos contain keywords, and you can add more later
  • Home network is fast
  • Never modify photo files, except moving them into their category folders

Adding photos

Copy your photos into the _process directory on the server(*), then click the Import menu item to add some initial keywords and start the import process.

You can also automate this process.
